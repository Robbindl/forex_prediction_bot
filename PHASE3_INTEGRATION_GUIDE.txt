════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TROUBLESHOOTING
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: "[OrderFlow] Subscribed to ORDER_BOOK_UPDATE" — but no walls detected
Phase 1 must be running and connected to an exchange for order book
updates to arrive. Confirm you see "[ExStream] binance connected" in logs.

Problem: No STOP_HUNT_DETECTED events
Stop hunts are rare events. The detector needs MIN_PRICE_HISTORY=15
ticks before it starts scanning, and then only fires on genuine
wick-and-revert patterns. This is working correctly.

Problem: Too many LIQUIDITY_WALL_DETECTED alerts
Increase WALL_ALERT_COOLDOWN_SECS in liquidity_wall_detector.py
(default 120 seconds). You can also raise WALL_MULTIPLIERS["MODERATE"]
from 5.0 to a higher value to only catch larger walls.
